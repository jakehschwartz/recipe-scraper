Recipe: 5 MinuteÂ Ice Cream Cake

Summary: This oh so easy ice cream cake can be made in minutes and with any flavors you wish!

Ingredients

  • 10 ice cream sandwiches
  • 1-2 flavors of ice cream, slightly softened
  • 12 Oreo cookies, finely crushed
  • Magic Shell ice cream topping

Instructions

  1. Line a 9×5″ loaf pan with heavy foil, letting it hang over on each side.
  2. Fit 5 sandwiches on bottom of pan. They should be snug.
  3. Spread layer of ice cream over-top of sandwiches, about 1-1 1/2″ thick.
  4. Fit remaining 5 sandwiches over ice-cream.
  5. Spread 2nd layer of ice cream over sandwiches.
  6. Cover ice-cream with crushed Oreo cookies.
  7. Cover with saran wrap and freeze until set, at least 2Â hours or overnight.
  8. To serve, invert cake onto plate or platter. Cover top with Magic Shell & cut into slices.
