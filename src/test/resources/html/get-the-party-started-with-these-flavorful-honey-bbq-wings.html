Get The Party Started With These Flavorful Honey BBQ Wings

Flavortown USA.

Alvin Zhou
BFMP Video Producer
Tasty
BuzzFeed Staff
Nick Guillory
BuzzFeed Social Media Strategist

1. Honey BBQ Chicken Wings

View this image ›

ID: 8452458

2. Here’s a video to show how it’s made:

Video available at: https://youtube.com/watch?v=_r1B365GxNY.
youtube.com
ID: 8452456

3. INGREDIENTS

1 cup flour
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
20 chicken wings or drumettes
1 cup BBQ sauce
½ cup honey

ID: 8452462

4. PREPARATION

Preheat oven to 425°F.

In a bowl, combine flour, chili powder, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Coat the wings in the flour evenly, shaking off any excess. Place the floured wings on a parchment paper–lined baking sheet and spread them out in a single layer. Bake for 45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until skin is crispy and golden brown.
Preheat oven to 500°F.

In a separate bowl, combine the BBQ sauce and the honey. Stir the cooked wings in the sauce and coat them evenly. Place the coated wings back onto the baking sheet and spread them in a single layer. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, until sauce is bubbly and caramelized. Cool, then serve!
