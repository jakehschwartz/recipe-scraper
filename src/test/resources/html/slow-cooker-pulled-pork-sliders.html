Yields 4

Mexican Pulled Pork Sliders

A simple Mexican flavored slow cooker recipe for pulled pork. Great on sliders or with rice and beans.

10 minPrep Time

4 hrCook Time

4 hr, 10 Total Time

Save Recipe
Print Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2- 2 lb. pork roast
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup green salsa (I used medium for some heat)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • salt & pepper
  • 8 whole wheat slider buns
  • cilantro for garnish (optional)
  • red cabbage, sliced for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium high heat, saute onions & garlic with olive oil.
  2. Season pork roast generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Once softened (about 5 minutes), push onion & garlic mixture to the sides and add the pork roast to the pan. Brown each side for about 2 minutes.
  4. Transfer pork roast with onion & garlic mixture to a warmed slow cooker.
  5. Add cumin, coriander and salsa.
  6. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours.
  7. Once cooked, place pork on cutting board and shred apart with two forks.
  8. Transfer shredded pork back into slow cooker and mix together with the salsa & onion mixture to coat.
  9. Toast slider buns, place pork on buns and garnish with cilantro & cabbage if using.
